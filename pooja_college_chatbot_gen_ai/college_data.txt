Admissions:
Students must have completed 12th grade with at least 60% marks.

Fees:
Tuition fee is $5000 per semester.

Hostel:
Hostel fee is $1200 per semester including mess.

Scholarships:
Merit scholarships are available for students scoring above 85%.

Library:
Library is open from 8 AM to 8 PM Monday to Saturday.

Attendance:
Minimum attendance required is 75%.

Examinations:
Students failing more than 3 subjects may face academic probation.

Placements:
Placement cell conducts training every semester.

Courses:
Available courses include Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Business Administration.

Contact:
Admission office email is admissions@college.edu.

Transport:
College buses are available on 15 routes across the city.

Cafeteria:
The cafeteria is open from 7 AM to 7 PM and serves vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals.

Sports:
Facilities include cricket ground, basketball court, football field, badminton court, and gymnasium.

Internships:
Students are encouraged to complete at least one internship before graduation.

WiFi:
Free WiFi is available across campus for all registered students.

Medical:
A health center with a full-time nurse and visiting doctor is available on campus.

Events:
Annual cultural fest and technical symposium are conducted every year.

Clubs:
Student clubs include coding club, robotics club, dance club, music club, and photography club.

Labs:
Computer labs are open from 9 AM to 6 PM on working days.
